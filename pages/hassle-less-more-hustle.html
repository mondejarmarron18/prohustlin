Hassle less, more hustle

Van Joseph De Ramos

Hustling is a slang word meant to describe the action of working excessively to achieve goals. Don't take this negatively! The new generation seeks to retire early and enjoy the fruits of their labour. Nobody is no longer waiting until they're 60 to retire. It is more like taking advantage of the vigour of youth while it's still full.

Hustle more now for a hassle-less future.

Gen Z's have experienced a challenging childhood with some parents barely making it paycheck-per-paycheck and going through food stamps to survive. Witnessing the death of loved one's caused by sedentary lifestyles. Survival is part of human nature, but so is becoming the fittest of them all. Gen Z's are through with only surviving. They want a better and longer life with peace, good health, and financial freedom on a different level.

Elon Musk and other billionaires glorify the hustle culture with their current lifestyle - usually explaining how they're relaxing now because of their sacrifices when they were just starting. Basically, sacrifice on sleep and socialization, barely sleeping through the night, and not going out for dinners or parties.

With the pandemic, health professionals are encouraging many to prioritize physical and mental health. Physical health should be improved by becoming conscious of what to eat and having a longer sleeping time. This concept contradicts the hustle culture. However, the hustle has shifted to seeking balance in hard work. As hustling is used to describe hard work, it shouldn't single out the thought of only working hard to earn money. Goals can also be seeking a healthier and sustainable lifestyle.

Hustle more now on becoming healthy for a hassle-less future.

Making conscious decisions on what to eat and when to exercise will minimize health risks in the future. Imagine what a hassle to spend all your earnings on endless medical bills because you chose an unhealthy lifestyle now. It will only take at least 25-30 minutes three times a week of exercise to maintain a healthy body in the future. It should also be paired with making healthy food options by simply following the food pyramid.

Hustle more now on improving your mindset and character for a hassle-less future.

Change is inevitable, and it is the key to growth. It is vital to constantly assess ourselves and make changes for improvement. Hustling now to improve ourselves will save us from hassle in the future, such as making financial decisions - saving and purchasing. Maintaining a sustainable lifestyle doesn't limit our spending. It is just being wise when spending, which is saving. Learning materials about financial literacy are abundant on the internet - learn them! It only takes reading and application of learning to maintain a sustainable lifestyle in the future. Spend wisely and save abundantly.

As a famous quote says, "I don't make excuses. I make results."

If you've reached this point of the blog, you are bound to the challenge of making your own hustle. Keep in mind to hustle for betterment. Start now!
